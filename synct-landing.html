How it works

Privacy in
three steps.

01
Verify with WhatsApp OTP
Sign up with your phone number. We send a one-time code via WhatsApp — no email, no password, no data to leak.
02
Keys generated on your device
Your encryption keys are created locally and never leave your phone. We hold public keys only — useless without your private key.
03
Messages encrypted before sending
Every message is encrypted on your device using the Signal Protocol. Our servers see only ciphertext — unreadable blobs.
